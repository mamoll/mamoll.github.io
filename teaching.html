Over the years, I have (co-)taught and (co-)organized several tutorials related to robot motion planning:

  • Lectures on sampling-based motion planning and its combination with task planning at the ICAPS 2016 Summer School.
  • Motion Planning for Mobile Manipulation Tutorial at ICRA 2013.
  • Robot Motion Planning for Real Robots Tutorial at IROS 2011.
  • Motion planning tutorial at IROS 2008.
